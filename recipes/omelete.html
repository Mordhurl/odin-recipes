Omelette

In this recipe i will teach all of you guys, how to prepare a really tasteful Omelette


Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon "farofa"
  • 1 tablespoon soybean oil

Steps

For the mass

  1. Mix the egg, salt and "farofa" in a bowl;
  2. Reserve the mixed ingredients

Cooking

  1. Grease the pan with the oil;
  2. Add the mixed ingredients
  3. Wait untill the bottom of the mass get some consistency
  4. Slowly mix the mass without breaking it
  5. Turn the mass and wait the other side get some consistency
  6. It's ready to eat !!!
