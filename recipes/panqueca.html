Pancakes

In this recipe i will teach all of you guys, how to prepare a evil pancake U+1F608


Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 4 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 tablespoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup wheat-flour
  • 1 tablespoon soybean oil
  • 1 tablespoon yeast

Steps

For the mass

  1. Mix the egg and the sugar in a bowl;
  2. Add the vanilla, the milk and the oil and mix;
  3. Sift the wheat-flour, then add in the bowl;
  4. Finally, add the yeast and slowly mix.

Cooking

  1. Grease the pan with a little bit of oil;
  2. Add the mass;
  3. Wait for some bubbles in the mass;
  4. Turn the mass;
  5. Wait for the mass to brown;
  6. Take out from the pan;
  7. It's ready to eat !!!
